Montreal Newsletter

The Montreal booklet is about the summer activities and events of May, June, and July.The newsletter shows the natural scenery in May, the musical concerts in June, and the delicious food in July.This project is teamwork where we are challenged to do individual research of the month, create master pages on Adobe InDesign, and build consistent design throughout the document.We separate the workload by having individuals design for their chosen month. I make the content and styling for July. I handle the technical part of building the file on InDesign.

  • Type of Project

    Group Project

  • Applications

    Adobe Photoshop

    Adobe Illustrator

    Adobe InDesign

  • Challenges

    We are three people working on this project and had a deadline to fulfil in a couple of weeks.We had one member who could not complete his part on time. The other member and I finished our part on time and had to inform our professor of the situation.

  • Solutions

    We end up working together to facilitate creating the content, layout, and design of the missing part. It was an unfortunate circumstance but rewarding because I end up learning working in a design team. I love the final result of the newsletter.
