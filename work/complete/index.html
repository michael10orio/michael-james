Complete App

The Complete application is a simple project management application that let users create an account, make a list of their project titles with their description, set its deadline, and archive or complete their project. Building the Complete app needed completing the back end such as the PHP Javascript. For the design, I needed bold sans-serif Archivo typography with dark colors. I also produced wireframes of the home, list, and message pages. For the branding, I created an animated and corporate logo. It was educative on creating documentation for the database and figuring out the queries.
