Scarcity Magazine

Scarcity Magazine is a publication of news about the art industry. The theme of this company is informative, unique, and artistic.The cover is elegant and rich which captures the meaning of a scarcity of historical and artistic resources.The magazine shows a consistent design and various layouts. It uses plain colors of black and white to make typography beautiful Bodoni and Gills Sans fonts stand out. It has huge images to showcase the artwork and be a part of the design on its pages.

  • Type of Project

    Magazine Layout

  • Applications

    Adobe InDesign

    Adobe Photoshop

  • Challenges

    I find that doing the research for the content of the magazine because I want to consist of variety and interesting news that is not covered by the mainstream media. I am challenged to create a creative layout for the content and images.

  • Solutions

    I find the solution by doing thorough research with different magazines.Then, I design a flexible spread that presents different layouts to support the content and photos. Overall, I love the result of the magazine because it has a simple and unique design.
