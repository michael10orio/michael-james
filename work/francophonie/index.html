Francophonie Event

This project is a poster contest for the “Semaine de la Francophonie” event for Vanier College. My poster represents creativity, open-mind, and educative which is shown by the girl sitting down and thinking out loud.The clouds are illustrations of French history, education, fashion, and leisures.The girl is an example of a college student.This made design helps people who are looking for activities to increase networking on campus. It makes the poster engaging and creative.

  • Type of Project

    Event Campaign

  • Applications

    Adobe Photoshop

  • Challenges

    The objective is to create a design that is an eye- catching, relatable, and informative poster. It was difficult to find photos and drawings that fit together and capture the theme of the event.

  • Solutions

    The result is t a composite that includes the history, games, fashion, and language of the French culture. The bright colors and creative illustrations make all the elements fit the subject of interconnection.
