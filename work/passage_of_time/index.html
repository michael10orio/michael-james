Passage of Time

This composite represents the passage of time of a man growing up and his favourite transport mobile from a bike to a skateboard and a car. Its concept consists of editing several pictures in one that involves using adjustment layers, masking layers, retouching, cropping, incorporating blend mode, and other essential Photoshop skills to make the photo realistic and artistic.This Photoshop project is a quality experience with clever and meaningful technical methods.

  • Type of Project

    Picture Composite

  • Applications

    Adobe Photoshop

  • Challenges

    I learn how to create realistic shadows in objects and people.This help make the element looks real and make them fit the background and characters. I am challenged to create different layers and adjustment layers.

  • Solutions

    I watch tutorials on making shadows and experiment with different blurs and effects on the elements. I group my files with names and organize my layers in order. I experience an easier on editing each photo.
