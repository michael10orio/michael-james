Michaels Chocolate

Michaels a chocolate box with its name inspired from yours truly. It is an old fashion chocolate brand that is elegant and creative.The design is a mix of art deco and art nouveau which is requirement of the project.This project contain creating several layout and 3D drawing in the Adobe Illustrator. It needs design in every side of the box.At the end of the digital process, it have to be print, cut, glued, and assemble.This print project is great creation that I am proud to have and keep for my portfolio.

  • Type of Project

    Packaging Box

  • Applications

    Adobe Illustrator

  • Challenges

    I was task to find a design that fit the chocolate company. I did thorough researched for the style as their was not many inspiration online. I had many attempts and errors of the design.

  • Solutions

    The solution is to assemble elements of what chocolate symbolize and the theme it represents. It represent from the form of the chocolate bars and trees of cocoa nuts. These are the illustration that I have an art nouveau and Deco style.
